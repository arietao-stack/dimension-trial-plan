Why the favorite gets 15%, not 40%

  • Pool depth: equestrian × TikTok Shop × <20k followers is a few hundred creators — overweighting stalls the daily quota.
  • Readout: ~70 videos ÷ 4 equal ways = no readable signal. 40/25/20/15 gives the lead angle statistical weight.
  • The Jul 26 gate is scored on total volume — broad angles carry it.
  • * Precision fallback: if section filters cannot isolate real riders, split Equestrian into its own From-Creator-List campaign (AI-searched saved list) and renormalize the rest to 47/29/24.

How the brief reaches creators

  • One invite for all four sections — the platform allows a single message per campaign: generic ≤500-char pitch, {Creator Name} variable, contact pr@cobasdaughter.com · "send to affiliate inbox" ON · "email creators" ON.
  • Angle brief goes out manually at approval: 4 condensed chat templates (Summer / Sports / Morning / Equestrian) — when approving a sample, paste the matching one. ~1 min each, ~8/day at target.
  • Follow-ups are automated: D+3 / D+7 via creator Messaging Tasks (part of the no-post loop).

No-post control — how 70% gets enforced

Dimension scores this natively: every creator carries a PPS score (0–5) and historical post rate, shown with an AI verdict at approval.
  • Approval gate: post rate ≥50% & PPS ≥4.0 — borderline must confirm a posting commitment in chat.
  • Chase: automated D+3 / D+7 follow-ups (creator Messaging Tasks) · one-sample rule until first video is live.
  • Weekly sweep: >10 days no video → final nudge → excluded from all future lists. Exposure caps at COGS ~$5–8.
