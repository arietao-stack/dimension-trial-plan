02 / 08

The mechanism

You build the campaign once.
The machine sends every day.

A campaign is a template: target filters + products + commission + one invitation message. Once live, Dimension auto-picks matching creators and sends invitations daily — nobody sends anything by hand.

The real bottleneck is 190 invites/day. The "2,000/week" quota is nominal — 190 × 7 = 1,330 is the true weekly ceiling. Quota resets Monday 2AM and unused days are lost forever: a day the machine doesn't send is 190 invites gone.

  • Invites only reach creators under 20k followers (TikTok cap at our shop tier). Bigger creators come in via Open Collab → Target Collab.
  • Daily human work is 15–20 min: approve sample requests, reply to creator messages. Not campaign creation.

Why the strategic favorite gets 15%, not 40%

  • Pool depth: equestrian × TikTok Shop × <20k followers × GMV $1k+ is a few hundred creators at best — overfunding it stalls the daily quota or dilutes the filter.
  • Readout: ~22 total videos split four ways = no angle has a readable sample. 40/25/20/15 gives the lead angle statistical weight while equestrian still yields a qualitative signal.
  • The Jul 26 gate is scored on total samples + request rate — the broad angles carry that number.

Equestrian scoreboard (not judged on 14-day GMV)

  • Curated list of ≥25–30 qualified equestrian creators built (AI vector search → saved list)
  • ≥12 samples accepted into the community · ≥5 videos with views ≥ creator's own median
  • "Where do I buy this" comments & saves — the real TOF signal
  • 2–3 ambassador / paid-collab candidates shortlisted for August
