No-post control — how 70% gets enforced. Dimension scores this natively: every creator carries a PPS score (0–5) and historical post rate, shown with an AI verdict at approval time; Samples analytics tracks sample → content per creator.
  • Gate at approval: only approve post rate ≥50% & PPS ≥4.0; borderline cases must confirm a posting commitment in chat first.
  • Chase: automated D+3 and D+7 follow-up after delivery (creator Messaging Tasks).
  • One-sample rule: no second SKU until the first video is live.
  • Weekly sweep: shipped >10 days + no video → final nudge, then exclude from all future lists.
  • Exposure is capped: a no-poster costs product COGS only (~$5–8); commission pays only on actual sales.
