Why equestrian gets 15%, not 40%

  • Pool depth: equestrian × TikTok Shop × <20k followers is a few hundred creators — overweighting stalls the daily quota.
  • Readout: ~70 videos split 4 equal ways reads as noise. 40/25/20/15 keeps the lead angle readable.
  • The Jul 26 gate is scored on total volume — broad angles carry it.
  • * Fallback: if filters can't find real riders, move Equestrian to its own From-Creator-List campaign and rebalance the rest to 47/29/24.

How the brief reaches creators

  • One invite for all four sections (platform limit): ≤500 chars, {Creator Name} variable, contact pr@cobasdaughter.com. Both send toggles ON.
  • The angle brief is pasted by hand at approval: 4 saved chat templates, pick the matching one. About 8 pastes a day.
  • Follow-ups run on their own: D+3 and D+7 via Messaging Tasks.
