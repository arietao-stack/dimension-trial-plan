Objective — prove the machine before paying for it. Dimension automates cold creator outreach for TikTok Shop. In 13 sending days we spend the full invitation quota across 4 audience angles, measure the sample-request funnel against the shop's own benchmarks, and decide before the first billing day. Success ≈ $10–12 per shipped sample vs ≈$540 per bought video.

01 How the mechanism works

STEP 1Build 4 campaigns, onceFilters + products + commission + one invite message. Jul 15. No daily rebuilding — automated campaigns self-refresh every Mon.
STEP 2Machine invites dailyHard cap 190/day = the real ceiling (190×7=1,330; the "2,000/wk" is nominal). Resets Mon 2AM. Unused days are lost.
STEP 3Creators request samplesOnly <20k-follower creators are invitable at our shop tier. Bigger ones enter via Open Collab → Target Collab.
STEP 4We approve — AI-vettedEach request shows PPS score, post rate, AI verdict. Auto-approve OFF. Requests expire ~4 days. Ops: 15–20 min/day.
STEP 5Brief travels as textNo PDF upload exists: 3-line pitch in the invite → condensed 1-page brief pasted in chat after approval. Designed brief pages stay internal.
STEP 6Videos post → GMVTagged shop-link videos tracked in Affiliate → Videos; revenue attributed 30 days. Benchmarks: $39/sample, ~37% post rate.
