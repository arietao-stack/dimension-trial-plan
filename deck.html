No-post control — how 70% gets enforced

Dimension scores this natively: every creator carries a PPS score (0–5) and historical post rate, shown with an AI verdict at approval.
  • Approval gate: post rate ≥50% & PPS ≥4.0 — borderline must confirm a posting commitment in chat.
  • Chase: automated D+3 / D+7 follow-ups (creator Messaging Tasks) · one-sample rule until first video is live.
  • Weekly sweep: >10 days no video → final nudge → excluded from all future lists. Exposure caps at COGS ~$5–8.
